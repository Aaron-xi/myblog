<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="🐳-1-部署-Docker-subs-check"><a href="https://vercel.blog.cmliussss.com/p/ProxyPool/#%F0%9F%90%B3-1-%E9%83%A8%E7%BD%B2-Docker-subs-check"></a>🐳 1. 部署 Docker&nbsp;<a target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener" href="https://github.com/beck-8/subs-check">subs-check</a></h2>

<!-- /wp:heading -->



<!-- wp:paragraph -->

<p><strong>subs-check 支持三种部署方式，用户可根据自己的操作习惯和硬件环境选择最适合自己的方式：</strong></p>

<!-- /wp:paragraph -->



<!-- wp:list {"ordered":true} -->

<ol class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->

<li><a href="https://vercel.blog.cmliussss.com/p/ProxyPool/#1-1-%E9%83%A8%E7%BD%B2%E6%96%B9%E5%BC%8F%E4%B8%80%EF%BC%9A%E5%B0%8F%E7%99%BD%E5%91%BD%E4%BB%A4%E4%B8%80%E9%94%AE%E9%83%A8%E7%BD%B2%E5%AE%B9%E5%99%A8">小白命令一键部署容器</a><br>适合新手，操作简单，但重置 Docker 后会<strong>丢失所有配置信息</strong>。</li>

<!-- /wp:list-item -->



<!-- wp:list-item -->

<li><a href="https://vercel.blog.cmliussss.com/p/ProxyPool/#1-2-%E9%83%A8%E7%BD%B2%E6%96%B9%E5%BC%8F%E4%BA%8C%EF%BC%9A%E4%BD%BF%E7%94%A8-docker-compose-yml-%E9%83%A8%E7%BD%B2%E5%AE%B9%E5%99%A8">使用 <code>docker-compose.yml</code> 部署容器</a><br>推荐方式，<strong>便于管理和持久化配置</strong>，适合大多数用户。</li>

<!-- /wp:list-item -->



<!-- wp:list-item -->

<li><a href="https://vercel.blog.cmliussss.com/p/ProxyPool/#1-3-%E9%83%A8%E7%BD%B2%E6%96%B9%E5%BC%8F%E4%B8%89%EF%BC%9A%E5%91%BD%E4%BB%A4%E8%A1%8C%E5%88%9B%E5%BB%BA-docker-%E5%AE%B9%E5%99%A8">命令行创建 <code>docker</code> 容器</a><br>适合熟悉 Docker 命令的用户，<strong>subs-check 配置和节点信息可保留</strong>，但<strong>无法保留 Docker 构建信息</strong>。</li>

<!-- /wp:heading -->



<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->

<h3 class="wp-block-heading" id="3-1-创建-CF-隧道，并获取隧道TOKEN"><a href="https://vercel.blog.cmliussss.com/p/ProxyPool/#3-1-%E5%88%9B%E5%BB%BA-CF-%E9%9A%A7%E9%81%93%EF%BC%8C%E5%B9%B6%E8%8E%B7%E5%8F%96%E9%9A%A7%E9%81%93TOKEN"></a>3.1 创建 CF 隧道，并获取隧道TOKEN</h3>

<!-- /wp:heading -->



<!-- wp:list {"ordered":true} -->

<ol class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->

<li>登录 <a href="https://dash.cloudflare.com/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">Cloudflare</a>，进入 <strong>Zero Trust</strong>；<br><a href="https://vercel.blog.cmliussss.com/img/ProxyPool/3-1-1.png"></a></li>

<!-- /wp:list-item -->



<!-- wp:list-item -->

<li>点击 <strong>网络</strong> → <strong>Tunnels</strong> → <strong>添加隧道</strong>；<br><a href="https://vercel.blog.cmliussss.com/img/ProxyPool/3-1-2.png"></a></li>

<!-- /wp:list-item -->



<!-- wp:list-item -->

<li>选择隧道类型：<code>Cloudflared</code>；<br><a href="https://vercel.blog.cmliussss.com/img/ProxyPool/3-1-3.png"></a></li>

<!-- /wp:list-item -->



<!-- wp:list-item -->

<li>为隧道命名，填入你运行<strong>docker设备的名称</strong>（如：<code>nas</code>），点击 <strong>保存隧道</strong>；<br><a href="https://vercel.blog.cmliussss.com/img/ProxyPool/3-1-4.png"></a></li>

<!-- /wp:list-item -->



<!-- wp:list-item -->

<li>点击<code>运行命令</code>复制命令，如下：</li>

<!-- /wp:list-item --></ol>

<!-- /wp:list -->
